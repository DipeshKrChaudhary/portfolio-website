Technology And Its Impacts

When we think of word technology, what comes in our mind? We might possibly think of roborts and
artificials intellegence from si-fi movies. It makes us to think of complex things, but its not that complex.

Technology is the collection of techniques, skills and process used in production of goods an
services. Technologies can be the use of scientifics knowledge for acccomishment of
objectives. In simple word, technology is the development and us of basic tools.

Technology Impacts

We all know that technology has a huge impact on all of our lives. We can notice how our lives are surrounded by gadget. The laptop at our desk, smart phone in our hands and wifi router install in home are some example how technology has change our life.

Technology changes on education

Technology has changes the way we learn. One of the most important things technology has done for students learning is to increase accessibility. Now students are no longer dependent on teachers, parents or other people to be only their source of information.

Changes in life style

As I already mention that it has huge impacts on our daily life. We can do anythings like shopping, comunicate with anywhere in the world through internet. Before people used to have times for friends and family but now-a-days we do have time for families and friends on social media.

Changes on other aspects

Now-a-days most of people toatlly depends on technology for almost everythings. They gain knowledge through internet but not by pratical and experirences. Every one needs to do search on google, we are fully dependent on internet. We can say that our next generation will come up with artifical intellegence.

Yes, technology has good impacts on our lives. On other hand, it has also negative aspects.
We human created the technology. So we are responsible for the negative impacts.
Technology is good or bad depend upon the user on how they utilize it.
